Noodles and Egg Recipe

How to make spicy noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 pack instant noodles (Indomietable or any brand)
  • 1–2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Pepper
  • Vegetables (carrot, green pepper, cabbage)

Instructions

  1. Pour 1 cup of water into a pot.Place it on medium heat and let it boil.Add your noodles.Allow it to parboil for 2–3 minutes.
  2. Dice your vegetables, grind your pepper and set aside.
  3. Pour your oil into a pan on low heat and let it heat up. Add your pepper and diced vegetables into the oil,add seasoning and let it cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour in your parboiled noodles, add a little water and let it steam for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Remove the noodles from the heat.In a separate pan, add a little oil.Crack in your egg and fry it sunny-side or fully fried.Serve on top of the noodles. Enjoy your meal!
