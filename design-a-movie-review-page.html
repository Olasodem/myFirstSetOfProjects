Movie Review: The Herd

The 2025 Nigerian film The Herd is an intense crime thriller and survival drama that explores the brutal reality of kidnapping and banditry in Nigeria. The story turns a joyous wedding celebration into a desperate fight for survival when the couple and their guests are ambushed and abducted by gunmen disguised as cattle herdsmen.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆(9.5/10)
